Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Silent Wisdom ("we", "us", or "our") operates automation tools for the Silent Wisdom content platform. This policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

To facilitate the uploading and management of content across social media platforms, we use official API Services provided by YouTube (Google), TikTok, and Meta (Facebook and Instagram). We collect the following types of information:

  • Authentication Data: We access user tokens via OAuth (Open Authorization) for each respective platform to allow our application to post content on your behalf.
  • Media Assets: Video files, images, and metadata (titles, descriptions, hashtags) provided by you for the purpose of publishing.
  • Profile Information: Basic profile information (such as account handles) provided by the APIs to identify which account the content is being posted to.

2. Use of Data

We use the collected data specifically to provide and maintain our Service, including:

  • Facilitating the "Direct Post" feature to TikTok.
  • Uploading videos and managing metadata on YouTube.
  • Publishing reels, posts, or stories to Facebook and Instagram.

We do not use this data for marketing, profiling, or any other purpose outside of the direct functional requirements of our automation tools.

3. Data Transfer and Disclosure

Your information, including Personal Data, is only transferred to the respective platform's servers (Google, TikTok, or Meta) through their official, encrypted APIs.

  • YouTube: Use of YouTube functionality is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Meta: Use of Facebook and Instagram functionality is governed by the Meta Privacy Policy.
  • TikTok: Use of TikTok functionality is governed by the TikTok Privacy Policy.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any other third-party entities.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your authentication tokens and session data only for as long as necessary to provide the requested service.

  • Revoking Access: You may revoke our access to your accounts at any time through the security settings of the respective platforms:
    • Google Security Settings (for YouTube)
    • TikTok Security Settings
    • Facebook App Settings (for Facebook and Instagram)
  • Data Deletion: Upon revoking access or requesting account deletion, all associated tokens and temporary media assets are removed from our systems.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us via our official YouTube channel "Silent Wisdom" or our associated social media profiles.
